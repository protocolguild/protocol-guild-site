Our membership has concluded its latest quarterly audit of funded members as of [May 22 2026](https://gov.protocolguild.org/proposals/33882645862178984509772307297938034237426661131911375149902583914862663246942). This, our 16th consecutive update reaffirms our institution's consistency and ability to self-regulate.

The member registry now includes a number of new contributor accounts as well as new alumni; creating a representative body of the developers and maintainers who work on client implementations, research, and upgrade delivery.

Protocol Guild's membership is now 196, with a net increase of 9 (+4.8%) members from 187 of last quarter. This quarter, the membership update was once again processed onchain via Protocol Guild's Agora-based DAO contracts. Readers will find a comprehensive list of the changeset below.
