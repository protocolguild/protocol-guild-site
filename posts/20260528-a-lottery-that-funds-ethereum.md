Protocol Guild has partnered with Megapot to turn lottery ticket purchases into recurring onchain funding for Ethereum's core protocol maintainers. Starting today, every Megapot ticket bought through Protocol Guild's referral link routes 10% of fees to the contributors who maintain Ethereum L1.

How it works
------------

Megapot runs a daily $1 million jackpot on Base. Tickets cost $1 in stablecoins. Of every ticket sold, 10% is paid as a referral fee to the address that brought the player in plus 10% of any winning tickets purchased. When you play through Protocol Guild's link, those funds are routed back to core protocol maintainers.

Megapot is built on Base, an Ethereum L2, and settles in stablecoins. Every ticket purchase is an Ethereum transaction. Every drawing is verifiable. The protocol returns 100% of ticket sales to winners, liquidity providers, and referrers rather than applying toward administrative overhead in the way traditional lotteries do.

How to participate
------------------
Play through Protocol Guild's link: [megapot.io/fund/pg](http://megapot.io/fund/pg)

Every ticket compounds into ongoing support for Ethereum L1 R&D. Share the link, embed it, post it; referral revenue accrues to the Guild for the lifetime of every referred player.
