Protocol Guild is thrilled to announce that [Devcon 8 in Mumbai](https://devcon.org/en/) is once again providing free tickets to our membership of Ethereum’s active core protocol contributors.

Devcon is where Ethereum’s ideas meet the people who make them real. It’s not just a conference - it’s a coming-together of builders, researchers, educators, designers, community organizers, and contributors from every corner of the ecosystem. As a core part of Ethereum’s roadmap, Protocol Guild members are very excited to be traveling from all over the globe to attend this event and mingle with the broader ecosystem.

Running from November 3 to 6 in Mumbai, India is an especially meaningful home for this year’s Devcon. It’s a chance to celebrate the communities across India that are already pushing the frontier - building open source infrastructure, advancing ZK identity systems, and strengthening coordination layers that help Ethereum grow in every direction. 

If you’ve been part of Ethereum for years - or you’re just discovering what the ecosystem can be - Devcon is your chance to learn from the best, meet the people behind the work, and bring that energy back to your own community.

Members can easily claim their tickets by verifying their Protocol Guild address in [Devcon’s ticket store](https://devcon.org/en/tickets/store/). 

**About Devcon**

Devcon is an annual global event organized by the Ethereum Foundation; a space to dive into open-source technology and meet the communities behind it. In November 2026 Devcon is coming to Mumbai, to recognize and champion what India is already building. Open source infrastructure, ZK identity systems, and coordination layers built in India power Ethereum globally. Get your tickets to Devcon 8 now!

Learn more: [Website](https://devcon.org) - [X](https://x.com/EFDevcon/)

**About Protocol Guild**

Protocol Guild is a peer-managed collective of Ethereum protocol contributors and the leading independent organization dedicated to sustainable funding for core development. Since 2022, Protocol Guild has distributed more than $38MM directly to the individuals stewarding Ethereum protocol upgrades and maintenance.
