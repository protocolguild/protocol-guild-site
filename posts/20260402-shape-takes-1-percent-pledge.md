Protocol Guild is honored to welcome Shape as the newest project to complete the 1% Pledge --- a growing norm that routes a portion of ecosystem value directly to the people responsible for scaling, securing, and maintaining Ethereum. Through this commitment, Shape is signaling long-term alignment with Ethereum's core contributors and the public infrastructure that underpins the protocol.

Shape is a NFT-focused Ethereum Layer 2 built on the OP Stack and part of the Optimism Superchain. Designed from the ground up for NFTs and onchain objects, Shape makes Ethereum accessible and economically rewarding for creators through its native Gasback program, which returns the majority of of sequencer fees to smart contract owners. This structural commitment to builder sustainability --- rather than a temporary incentive --- reflects a broader philosophy that the value generated onchain should flow back to those building on it.

Beyond the Gasback program, Shape is growing a creator-aligned ecosystem that includes partnerships with OpenSea, Manifold, Highlight, and leading onchain artists. With over 35 million transactions processed and a token distribution that allocates 70% of the total $SHAPE supply to the community, Shape has positioned itself as one of the most creator- and community-weighted networks in the Superchain. Its security model inherits directly from Ethereum, keeping the two networks intrinsically linked.

Shape's participation in the 1% Pledge reflects a conviction that the cultural and economic vitality of onchain creation depends on the health of Ethereum's base layer. By committing 1% of the $SHAPE token supply to Protocol Guild, Shape ensures that part of the value flowing through its ecosystem returns to the core contributors who make that foundation possible. This creates a positive feedback loop: a stronger, more reliable Ethereum enables more expressive and vibrant creator economies, which in turn help sustain the protocol itself.

About Shape Shape is a creator-focused Ethereum Layer 2 built on the OP Stack and part of the Optimism Superchain. Shape is purpose-built for NFTs and onchain objects, offering EVM equivalence at a fraction of mainnet cost while inheriting Ethereum's security directly.

Shape's native Gasback program routes the majority of sequencer fees back to smart contract creators, making it the most financially rewarding chain for builders in the creator economy. With community-first tokenomics --- 70% of total $SHAPE supply allocated to the community --- Shape is designed to put value in the hands of the people who make it.

Learn more: [shape.network](https://shape.network/)

About Protocol Guild Protocol Guild is a peer-managed collective of Ethereum protocol contributors and the leading independent organization dedicated to sustainable funding for core development. Since 2022, Protocol Guild has distributed nearly $36MM directly to the individuals stewarding Ethereum protocol upgrades and maintenance.

Funding streams from the 1% Pledge vest over four years and are distributed to Protocol Guild's 187 current members according to transparent weighting and membership processes.
