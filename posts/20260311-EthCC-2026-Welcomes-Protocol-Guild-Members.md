Protocol Guild is grateful to announce that [EthCC[9]](https://ethcc.io/) (Mar 30 - Apr 2 2026) will be providing tickets to our members, recognizing the essential role that core contributors play in building and maintaining the infrastructure that makes the entire Ethereum ecosystem possible.

This generous gesture ensures that the individuals behind protocol upgrades, client implementations, and research can participate in one of Ethereum's most important community gatherings. This kind of support strengthens the feedback loop between the researchers and implementers building Ethereum and the broader community that depends on their work.

If you are a member, please check your email or the Discord to claim.

### About EthCC

EthCC (Ethereum Community Conference) is one of the largest and longest-running annual Ethereum conferences in Europe, bringing together developers, researchers, and community members for multiple days of talks, workshops, and collaboration. EthCC has been a cornerstone gathering for the Ethereum ecosystem since its inception.

Learn more at their 2026 event at [ethcc.io](https://ethcc.io).

### About Protocol Guild

Protocol Guild is a peer-managed collective of Ethereum protocol contributors and is the leading independent organization dedicated to sustainable funding for core development.

Funding streams from the 1% Pledge vest over four years and are distributed directly to Protocol Guild's 187 current members according to transparent weighting and membership processes. Since 2022, Protocol Guild has distributed $37MM directly to the individuals stewarding protocol upgrades and maintenance.

Learn more: [PG Website](https://www.protocolguild.org/)
