Protocol Guild is thankful to share that ETHPrague 2026 will be offering complimentary tickets to our members - a welcome recognition of the core contributors whose day-to-day work keeps Ethereum's infrastructure intact.

By welcoming the people behind client implementations, protocol upgrades, and research, ETHPrague helps close the distance between those building the base layer and the wider community that builds on top of it. We're grateful for this kind of recognition.

If you are a member, please check your email or the Discord to claim.

### About ETHPrague

ETHPrague is an annual Ethereum hackathon and conference set in Prague, Czech Republic. It has earned a reputation as one of the most technically substantive events in the ecosystem --- a gathering where serious builders show up to collaborate, debate, and ship.

Learn more at [ethprague.com](http://ethprague.com)

### About Protocol Guild

Protocol Guild is a peer-managed collective of Ethereum protocol contributors and is the leading independent organization dedicated to sustainable funding for core development.

Funding streams from the 1% Pledge vest over four years and are distributed directly to Protocol Guild's 187 current members according to transparent weighting and membership processes. Since 2022, Protocol Guild has distributed $37MM directly to the individuals stewarding protocol upgrades and maintenance.

Learn more: [protocolguild.org](http://protocolguild.org)
