Protocol Guild is thrilled to announce that the Aztec Foundation - stewards of the first decentralized, privacy-preserving Layer 2 on Ethereum - has joined an exclusive list of projects taking the Protocol Guild Pledge by donating 1% of the AZTEC token supply to Ethereum's core protocol contributors.

The tokens have been [deposited](https://etherscan.io/tx/0x62781cec7ebf7dd083754dec1de40cdc57f4d25034d2bfde70ffd48404d92b44) into Protocol Guild’s [immutable 4-year vesting contract](https://explorer.splits.org/accounts/0x25941dc771bb64514fc8abbce970307fb9d477e9/), providing long-term incentives to the stewards building and maintaining Ethereum’s core protocol. Today that group includes 187 individuals across client implementations, research teams, and coordination and support groups.

With this contribution, the Aztec Foundation is bolstering a growing norm of demonstrating long-term support for Ethereum's core contributors and the public infrastructure underpinning the protocol. As a privacy-first Layer 2 zkRollup on Ethereum, Aztec has a unique dependency on Ethereum’s core protocols development, while vice versa Ethereum has a dependency on rollups like Aztec to provide differentiated blockspace to attract new users. To that end, while Ethereum makes everything publicly visible, Aztec brings privacy to smart contracts, enabling confidential transactions and private state. Using Noir, a Rust-like programming language developed by Aztec Labs, developers can implement privacy at the user, data, metadata, transaction, and code levels without requiring deep cryptographic expertise.

The Aztec Foundation's completion of the 1% Pledge reflects a conviction that privacy is a foundational requirement for a credibly neutral Ethereum. Donating a portion of AZTEC tokens to Protocol Guild helps return value from Aztec’s ecosystem to the core contributors who maintain the base layer, creating a positive feedback loop: a more reliable, expressive Ethereum enables richer privacy applications, which in turn help sustain the protocol.

## About Aztec Foundation

The Aztec Foundation is a nonprofit organization that supports the growth and development of open-source programmable privacy. As the steward of the Aztec Network, the Foundation conducts fundamental research in freedom-enhancing cryptography, supports builders developing privacy-preserving applications, and maintains Noir, the universal language for zero-knowledge proofs.

The Aztec Network is the first fully decentralized, privacy-preserving Layer 2 zkRollup on Ethereum. It enables programmable privacy for smart contracts, allowing developers to build confidential applications without compromising decentralization or composability with Ethereum. Noir gives blockchain and web developers a powerful, general-purpose language for building privacy-preserving applications.
