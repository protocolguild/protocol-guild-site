Protocol Guild members spend their working hours hardening Ethereum's mainnet. The tools they rely on in their personal lives should reflect the same values they bring to their work: open source, self-custodial, and uncompromising on security.

We're glad to share that the[  Institute of Free Technology](https://free.technology/) is sponsoring a[  Keycard Shell](https://keycard.tech) bundle; one Shell device plus two Keycards; for every Protocol Guild member.

Keycard Shell is, by design, the most open hardware wallet shipping today: fully open-source firmware and hardware, air-gapped operation via QR codes, on-device transaction verification (no blind signing), signing performed inside a secure element, replaceable battery and a Duress PIN for plausible deniability. It works with over 15+ software wallets so you are free to use it with the wallets you are already comfortable with. 

That ethos lines up directly with the kind of work Protocol Guild members do every day. It feels right to equip the contributors maintaining the commons with hardware built on the same principles.

**About Keycard**

Keycard is a discreet, convenient, affordable hardware security solution for crypto assets. Taking the form of a credit card, Keycard offers comparable crypto security at a fraction of the cost to others in the hardware wallet niche. Keycard Shell is a modular air-gapped hardware wallet. Shell is a companion device for Keycard, allowing for signed transactions via QR codes and use with more software wallets.

Learn More: [Website](https://shell.keycard.tech/)

**About Protocol Guild**

Protocol Guild is a peer-managed collective of Ethereum protocol contributors and the leading independent organization dedicated to sustainable funding for core development. Since 2022, Protocol Guild has distributed more than $38MM directly to the individuals stewarding Ethereum protocol upgrades and maintenance.
